& Cost

🔥 10X Chromium Single Cell library preparation

The 10X single cell RNA-seq could only sequenced for 5’ or 3’-end transcript about 300-400bp, not full length capture.

[[[[ Example of Chromium Single Cell 3’ v2 library preparation ]]]]

& Procedure for library preparation 🔥🔥
  1. Reverse transcribe the full-length transcript [3’ assay or 5’assay]
  2. Amplifying the full-length cDNA
  3. Randomly fragmented under conditions that favor 300-400 bp length fragments. [Only the 3 or 5 end of transcript will be kept for next step]
  4. End repair and Truseq adapters R2 ligation
  5. Followed by clean up, the proper-size reads will be amplified during the Sample Index PCR.
  6. The final library pass to illumina or other platform for NGS sequencing.
& Cell Ranger for 10X

Cell Ranger is a set of analysis pipelines that process Chromium single-cell RNA-seq output to align reads, generate feature-barcode matrices and perform clustering and gene expression analysis.

& Cell Ranger correct for amplification bias

Unique Molecular Identifier [UMI]

After sequencing, the UMI is used to distinguish sequenced reads that originate from unique mRNA molecules vs PCR duplicates.

Multiple reads that match the same UMI, 10x barcode and gene are collapsed to a single UMI count in the gene-barcode UMI count matrix.

  • Single cell RNA-sequencing [10X Genomics]

  • Spatial Transcriptomics: The Visium Spatial Gene Expression Solution from 10x Genomics

    [Map the Whole Transcriptome Within the Tissue Context] using 10X Chromium

  • Single Cell CNV [single cell genome sequencing]

  • Scale target screening: Detecting sgRNA and perturbed gene expression profiles in each cell. [10X]

    @ need the Single Cell 3’ v3 Gel Beads [poly (dT) + Two additional capture sequences]

    @ To enable direct capture, each sgRNA should be engineered to contain either Capture Sequence 1 or Capture Sequence 2, along with a protospacer (Feature Barcode), designed to target gene/s of interest.

    @ Performing sgRNA QC by qPCR, NGS or other methods is recommended before proceeding with 10x Genomics Single Cell Solutions. [sgRNA will be captured by primer on-bead]

  • Combining Gene expression profiling and cell surface protein detection with tens to hundreds of antibodies. [10X]

  • LIBRA-seq (linking B cell receptor to antigen specificity through sequencing) [10X]
