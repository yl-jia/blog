※ Reminder

  • All PhD students are obligated to follow the wholesome of BMIC days.(Checked by answering the Quiz during sessions).

  • Talks = 10 min presentation + 5 min question [Jury members + Audience]

    Flash talks = 3min presentation + 5min question [Only Jury members]

  • External guests [career] = 15-20min + 15-20min question [Q&A then we ask for u ]

    Postdoc express = 10min question [Q&A then we ask for u]

  • Quiz session: just relax and think about the presentations ! The answers are hidden in there! [*choice question*] Answer as much you can increase your chance to be one of our lucky 12 winners ! [Prizes worth 50€ - Kadeoo gift card]
